Random Access Memories - Daft Punk

After the French House duo announced their separation, I decided to take a second look at their star-studded last album.

This album is a very unique entry in the Daft Punk catalog. The duo turned away from their typical house production to create a more tradition disco and pop sound. Daft Punk also collaborated with many legendary musicians including Nile Rogers of Chic, Pharrell Williams, and Julian Casablances of The Strokes to name a few. Overall, the album is one of they've best ever made.
